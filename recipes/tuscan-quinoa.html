Tuscan Quinoa Salad

Fresh and packed full of protein!

This simple dish will leave you full and refreshed. Serve it as a side dish, put it in a wrap, or have a little extra to make it a meal on it's own.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups quinoa
  • 3 cups water
  • 1(15 ounce) can white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1(6 ounce) jar sun dried tomatoes, drained
  • 4 cups spinach
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/4 cup lemon fresh juice
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Directions:

  1. Combine quinoa and water in pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, reduce heat and cook for about 20 minutes uncovered on low heat.
  3. Remove pot from heat and cover with lid.
  4. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  5. Remove lid and fluff quinoa
  6. Add fluffy cooked quinoa to a large bowl
  7. Add sun-dried tomatoes (drained but oil reserved), cheese, spinach and beans to the bowl and stir to combine.
  8. Whisk together lemon juice, reserved oil, salt and pepper to make dressing.
  9. Pour dressing over quinoa salad and incorporate into the rest of the ingredients.
  10. Enjoy in a wrap sandwich, as a side dish or as is!
